SPROUT

Sprout is a game that has been made to delve into the topics of change in our own world, how to survive in dire situations, and about teamwork. This game is suitable for those 5 years old and up.

In this game there has been a bomb that has gone off in the city you live in. It is your task to survive and build you and the team members you find along the way a sustainable way of living. Each team member being unique and interesting in their own ways it is for sure that you will grow to love these characters along your adventure together.

LUMBER FOREST

Lumber Forest is a game that explores grief. This game is suitable for ages 5 and up.

As players go through this game is your goal to help all of the creatures you come across in need of help. You travel along with your friend Bear and they help protect and provide for you as well. This game shows a bit more of how the different stages of grief are shown or how they are processed.

HUMAN

Human is a game that has a deeper dive into heavier topics in a more upfront way. The game is not gruesome but it holds actual discussion of heavier topics.

THIS GAME IS NOT SUITABLE FOR YOUNG CHILDREN

This game is suitable for children 14 years old and older.

As you play you are working to help people through different stages of their lives and to further understand the complexity that each individual holds. While also including the beauty of the world around us and in people in our lives. The balance between heavier discussion and humorous moments brings a thoughtful and heartwarming game to the player.
